Breaks down customer profitability by gender.

4. Profit by Product Color

Identifies high-selling and high-profit colors for optimization.

5. Profit by Pricing Types

Evaluates how different price categories influence profit.

6. Country-wise Profit Map

A custom geographic visualization showing profit distribution by country.

Segments customers into age groups to analyze demographic profitability.
